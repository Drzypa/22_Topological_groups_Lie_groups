\begin{definition}
The \emph{category $Rep(\grp)$ of representations} has objects the representations of a groupoid $\grp$, and as morphisms the \emph{intertwiners} $i : \rho_j \longrightarrow \rho_k$  that are (vector) bundle morphisms $i:E \longrightarrow E$ over the manifold $M$ so that $\rho_k(g) \circ i = i \circ \rho_j$. Because representations are functors $\rho: \grp \longrightarrow {\bf Vect}$, an itertwiner $i$ is in fact a natural transformation between two such functors that are groupoid representations of $\grp$, in this case implemented {\it via} the vector bundle morphisms $i: E \longrightarrow E$. 
\end{definition}
